Tucson Pathways

Finding Safer, Smarter, Quieter Cycling Routes

our mission.

If you are interested in riding a bicycle but apprehensive due to safety you are not alone. In fact, you're in the majority. Riding a bicycle is an incredible positive feedback loop. The impacts are direct and measurable to your health, the environment, your finances, your mental state, and community wellness.

But at its core, Tucson is still a car-centric city those safety concerns keep ridership low and the barrier of entry high. Our focus is to challenge that barrier of entry, by highlight solutions hidden in plain sight. We hope to empower potential bicycle riders, to reimagine Tucson as neighborhoods connected by crosswalks, rather than roadways connected by intersections.
