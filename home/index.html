our mission.

Riding a bicycle is an incredible positive feedback loop. The impacts are direct and measurable to your health, environment, finances, mental wellbeing, and your community's development.

However most Tucsonans don't consider bicycles for transportion due to safety concerns. Most people want to ride more, but remain apprehensive due to the perceived danger of biking through a 'car-centric' city. This perception keeps ridership low and the barrier of entry high.

Our goal is to challenge how we percieve our city & navigate through it - beginning by envision our city as neighborhoods connected by crosswalks, rather than roadways connected by intersections.

why ride?

health.

Riding improves blood pressure, insulin levels, cholesterol levels; and conversely reduce risk of cancer, heart disease, and diabetes.

If your drive to work is ~20 minutes, biking will only require you to leave 15 minutes earlier, and result in more than 1 hour of exercise by the end of every workday.

Exchanging your daily time spent sitting during a drive, for time spent bike commuting, is one of the healthiest upgrade to daily activity one can make without having to change larger behavioral habits

Assumes non-highway (inter-city) commutes

environment.

Every mile you choose to bike over driving will prevent 1 pound of CO2 from going into the atmosphere. Roughly 3/4 of that pound will become carbonic acid in our oceans over the next 20 to 200 years as it reacts with ocean water.

Source 1 / Source 2 / Source 3

* Assumes 20mpg car.

financial.

A majority of car costs occur come from ownership, not useage. Whether you drive it or not, purchase price, insurance, registration, parking, depreciation already establish a financial commitment before you even factor in usage, maintenance and gas.
There is no doubt that a car can unlock a level of convenience in city-transport, but if you can offset your need for a car for even 6 months, the money saved on insurance alone can justify a $500-600 bicycle purchase.
A well maintained used bicycle maintains its re-sale value much longer than a used car. This makes it much easier to try commuting, and if its not for you. And if not you can recoup your funds by selling a bicycle on craigslist much faster than re-selling a car.

mental.

Similar to meditation - bike commutes are times in which you can 'check-out' and be among your thoughts.

other.

You will develop an improved resilience to bad weather, and greater apprecation of good weather.

You will know exactly how long your commutes take.

Riding your bike can be a launching board to other healthy habits you didn't know you were capable of.
